HR Sponsorship Transfer

This apps is useful when any expats candidate leave from other organization and hire on our organization at that time we need to change the sponsorship of candidate.

Submit Sponsorship Request

After hire any expats candidate, Recruitment Officer will put request for Sponsorship transfer with details of Employee Name, Recruiter, Handled By.



Send mail for required document

Once Recruitment Manager will submit request of sponsorship transfer is forward to HR department for Send Mail for required document to the employee.



HR Department Approval

Once HR Manager will receive required documents, approve the sponsorship transfer request.
